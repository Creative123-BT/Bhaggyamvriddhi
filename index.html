Building Trust, Delivering Excellence Since 1985

What began as a humble endeavor with a small team of expert engineers in 1985 has evolved into Bhaggyam—a name synonymous with quality and trust in Chennai’s construction industry.

From crafting elegant residential bungalows to iconic commercial complexes, hospitals, schools, and industrial buildings, we’ve left our mark alongside some of the finest architects.

Our commitment to excellence has earned us the loyalty of our clients, who repeatedly entrust us with their projects. As demand grew, we expanded into flat promotions, successfully delivering over 500 premium apartments and establishing ourselves as renowned residential builders in Chennai.

At Bhaggyam, learning never stops. We continuously refine our approach, innovate to enhance quality, and ensure customer satisfaction remains at the heart of all we do.
